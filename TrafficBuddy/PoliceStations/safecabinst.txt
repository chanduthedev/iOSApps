Safe Cab is a initiative to bring transparency, accountability & safety in riding cabs<br/><br/>
1. Scan the QR code or enter police id number on the cab registration certificate or enter vehicle number.
<br/><br/>2. View the cab details & reviews by other riders.
<br/><br/>3. Share your cab details with friends/family so that they are informed.<br/><br/>
4. Submit review about your ride.
<br/><br/>Help everyone & get helped by submitting reviews. For the people, By the people.
<br/><br/>All the reviews are anonymously monitored by officials and action is taken against cabs.
